What you'd gain switching from A → E1 or E2: per-symbol price + change %, live equity sparkline per symbol, gauge-ring probability indicators (more visually obvious than bars), bigger readable numbers.

What stays identical: the KPI strip metrics (Master Equity, Open P&L, Active Trades, Paper WR/PF, Models Deployed, APX Target), the event stream content + filters + pause, the regime indicator per symbol, the specialist model name, threshold-tick markers on probability bars, FIRE LONG/FIRE SHORT/WAIT badges, ON/OFF per-symbol pill, MINI↔MICRO toggle.

What stays absolutely untouched (below the terminal): the four full Futures Funded Account cards with all the prop-account details you see in your current screenshot — that whole section is unrelated to this choice.

My pick remains E1 — best balance of card detail and always-visible stream. But if you spend most of your time reading the stream (not the cards), E2 is the right call.
